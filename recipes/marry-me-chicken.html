Marry Me Chicken

Description

Marry me Chicken is a delicious, creamy chicken dish that's earned its romantic name for being so irresistibly good that it might just inspire a proposal! This recipe features tender chicken breasts simmered in a rich sauce made from garlic, sun-dried tomatoes, heavy cream and Parmesan cheese. The combination of flavors is both elegant and comforting, making it perfect for special occasions or a cozy weeknight dinner. With just the right balance of creamy, tangy, and savory notes, this dish is sure to impress anyone at the table.

What makes Marry Me Chicken truly appealing is its simplicity and versatility It pairs beautifully with a variety of sides like mashed potatoes, rice, or even pasta, soaking up the flavorful sauce for a complete, satisfying meal. Whether you're cooking for a loved one or treating yourself to a restaurant-quality dinner at home, this recipe is sure to become a go-to favorite. Get ready to make a meal that will leave a lasting impression!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes (drained and chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basil or parsley (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then sear in olive oil over medium heat until golden brown on both sides. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, saut√© minced garlic until fragrant, then add chicken broth, heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, and sun-dried tomatoes. Stir well.
  3. Add thyme and oregano, then bring the sauce to a simmer.
  4. Return the chicken to the pan, spoon the sauce over it, and let it cook on low heat for 10-15 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley and serve with your choice of sides. Enjoy!
